Lizzy McAlpine

A popular indie musician and artist known for her emotional songwriting and captivating sound.

About Lizzy

Elizabeth "Lizzy" McAlpine is an American singer-songwriter born on October 13, 1999, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Known for her deeply emotional and introspective songwriting, Lizzy blends indie folk, pop, and alternative sounds into a style entirely her own.

Her music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, nostalgia, and self-discovery. With a voice that feels both intimate and powerful, Lizzy has carved out a unique space in the modern indie music landscape.

Albums & Discography

  1. Give Me a Minute (2020)
    • Her debut album released independently
    • Features fan favorites like "Older" and "What a Terrible Thing to Do"
    • Established her signature intimate, folk-pop sound
    • Gained massive traction on Spotify and TikTok
  2. five seconds flat (2022)
    • Her major label debut via Arista Records
    • Features the viral hit "ceilings" which took over TikTok
    • Debuted at #28 on the Billboard 200
    • Praised by critics for its emotional depth and production quality
  3. Older (2024)
    • Her most mature and critically acclaimed work to date
    • Features "Come Down Soon" and "Drunk, Running"
    • Received widespread critical praise across music publications

Accomplishments

  1. Streaming Success

  3. Live Performances
